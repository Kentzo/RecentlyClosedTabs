How to use

Open Safari and choose View→Cusomize Toolbar…

Whenever customization panel would appaer locate the RecentlyClosedTabs button.

Drag the RecentlyClosedTabs button to the toolbar.

Whenever you want to see your recently closed tabs press the button.

Every closed tab is added to the history of recently closed tabs.

Use search bar in order to filter a content.

To reopen website select appropriate row and press Enter. Also you can do it by making Double Click on the row.

Columns of the table can be reorded

Click the header of a column in order to reorder a content
